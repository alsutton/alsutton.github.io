Droidcon London 2013

  • Thu, Jun 20, 2013
  • One-minute read

I got a call from Skills Matter asking me if I’d be interested in going to droidcon London 2013…. well, it’s already certain I’ll be there, and here’s why;

There are going to be several tracks at #DroidconUK13 , and each track has a track-master, a person who has volunteered to co-ordinate the track, sort out who talks in which slots, and make sure their track is appealing to developers.

One of those tracks is a Games track. And the track-master for the games track is….. Yup…. Me :).

This isn’t part of my work with OUYA, this is something I’m taking on personally, so I’m open to speakers whatever they’re working on and whoever they working for.

I’ve got a few people lined up to speak already, but I’m looking for cool speakers who want to talk about Games on Android, so if you’ve got something cool and exciting, and you want to talk at #DroidconUK13 , then all you need to do is fill in the “Request for Papers” form and you’ll be in the running….

Let the fun commence :)
