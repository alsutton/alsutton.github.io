Combining My Companies

  • Thu, Apr 28, 2011
  • One-minute read

I’ve been split between running two companies for the last couple of years; Enterprise Data Safe Ltd. and Funky Android Ltd., and while the two operate in different technology areas it’s been a bit tough to keep both plates in the air, so I’ve decided to buy out my co-founder in Enterprise Data Safe and combine the two.

Funky Android Ltd. will be the company which exists beyond the merger, and it will honour all the existing commitments of Enterprise Data Safe, so most folk won’t notice any change.
